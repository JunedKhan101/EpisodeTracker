Episode Tracker

This website allows you track how many episodes you've watched in a series.

Your streaming site may already have this feature but it's not that reliable because they are stored in browsers (session/cookies, browser cache, browser's local Storage/session Storage). All of which can get easily deleted if your browser gets updated, reinstalled or if you're watching on a different browser or on a different machine.

There are some TV series that are notoriously long. It's easy to get lost and forget where were you, this is where our website comes in rescue.

You can create/add series here, each series can have multiple seasons if you want to track series with multiple seasons just check "My series contain multiple seasons" when you add a series doing so would allow you to track multiple seasons separately, unchecking this option would allow you to track episodes within series directly.
