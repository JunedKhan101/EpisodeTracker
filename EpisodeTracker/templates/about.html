Episode Tracker Web App

This website allows you track how many episodes you've watched in a series.

Your streaming site may already have this feature but it's not that reliable because they are stored in browsers (session/cookies, browser cache, browser's local Storage/session Storage). All of which can get easily deleted if your browser gets updated or if you're watching on a different browser or on a different machine

There are some TV series that are notoriously long. It's easy to get lost and forget where were you, this is where our website comes in rescue.

For each series there is a separate folder.
